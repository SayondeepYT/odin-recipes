🐟 Fish Curry

⬅ Back to Home

🧂 Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Fish
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Turmeric
  • Potato

👨‍🍳 Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot.
  2. Slice onions.
  3. Add onions and cook until golden.
  4. Add fish pieces.
  5. Add salt and turmeric.
  6. Cook until fish turns reddish.
  7. Add potato slices.
  8. Add water and adjust seasoning.

📖 About This Dish

Fish curry is a popular Indian dish made with different types of fish like Hilsa (Elish), Rohu (Rui), Katla, and more. It is rich in flavor and widely enjoyed across regions.
