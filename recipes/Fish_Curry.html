How to make Fish Curry
  1. Take the oil in a pot and warm it.
  2. Slice the Onion in pieces.
  3. After warming the oil put the pieces of onions and cook it.
  4. While cooking take the pieces of fishes and put it ina pot.
  5. Mix Salt and turmeric as your taste suggests.
  6. You need to add the fishe in pot and cook it until it gives a red vibes
  7. Put the slices of potatoes in the pot and cook it.
  8. Lastly put water and taste for accurate taste , you can add extra salt for good taste
