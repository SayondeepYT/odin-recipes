📖 About This Dish

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese. It’s hearty, flavorful, and perfect for family meals.

🧂 Ingredients

  1. Meat: Italian sausage and ground beef
  2. Onion & Garlic: Adds rich flavor
  3. Tomato Products: Crushed tomatoes, sauce, paste
  4. Sugar: Enhances sauce flavor
  5. Spices: Basil, parsley, salt, pepper
  6. Noodles: Lasagna sheets
  7. Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta
  8. Egg: Helps bind cheese mixture

👨‍🍳 Cooking Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  3. Prepare ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the ingredients.
  5. Cover and bake.
  6. Let it rest before serving.

🧱 Layering Order

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella
  5. Repeat layers
  6. Top with Parmesan

⏱ Cooking Time

Bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 50 minutes. Cover for the first 25 minutes, then uncover. Let it rest for 15 minutes before serving.
